About Me

Hi, I'm Mukul, a video editor, 3D animator, and Motion Graphic Designer with 3 years of experience.

With expertise in Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Blender, I create visually impactful content that tells compelling stories. My journey from Microbiology to digital media has given me a unique perspective and the ability to deliver high-quality, engaging visuals.

I'm passionate about using my skills to craft memorable experiences and push creative boundaries. I enjoy photography, fitness, and taking inspiration from films.

Over the years, Ive worked with various clients and brands, creating motion graphics and video edits that stand out. I enjoy refining details, enhancing visuals, and making animations flow seamlessly

Every project is an opportunity to push creative boundaries. My goal is to ensure high-quality results great and communicate a clear message. Im always exploring new techniques and trends to keep my work fresh, engaging, and meaningful.

My Stack

Premier Pro

My go-to for video editing, where I refine footage, add transitions, adjust colors, and bring my creative vision to life with precise controls and powerful tools.

After Effects

I use it to create motion graphics, visual effects, and smooth animations, making my videos more dynamic and visually engaging with professional-level compositions.

DaVinci Resolve

An excellent tool for advanced color grading, precise editing, and professional post-production, helping me achieve cinematic-quality visuals in my projects.

Adobe Audition

My choice for audio editing and sound design, allowing me to clean up recordings, enhance voiceovers, and mix tracks for a polished and professional audio experience.

Photoshop

I design thumbnails, graphics, and visual assets, using powerful editing tools to create high-quality visuals that elevate the look of my videos and projects.

Illustrator

Perfect for creating vector graphics, icons, and logos, helping me design high-resolution assets that maintain clarity and sharpness at any scale.

Blender

I explore 3D modeling, animation, and rendering, using it to create visually striking elements and animations that enhance the depth of my projects.

Notion

Keeps my creative workflow organized, helping me plan projects, manage tasks, and document ideas efficiently, ensuring that everything stays structured.

Ai Tools

I experiment with AI-powered tools to enhance my work, whether it's generating creative ideas, automating tasks, or improving efficiency in editing and design.
